I am currently a first-year Pre-Doctoral Research Assistant in the Spatial Economics lab @ the Institute of Economics, Academia Sinica (IEAS), where I work with Professor Wen-Tai Hsu. I am broadly interested in the effects of public transportation, specifically how the addition of a bus or rail line affects a local economy's rent prices, migration, and job creation. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Economics from The Ohio State University in 2024. [cv]
