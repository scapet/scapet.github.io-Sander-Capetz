I am currently a first-year Pre-Doctoral Research Assistant in the Spatial Economics lab @ Academia Sinica, where I work with Wen-Tai Hsu. I am broadly interested in the effects of public transportation, specifically how the new addition of a bus or rail line affects a local economy's rent prices, migration, and job creation.
